Office Online Applications Comparative

Microsoft Onedrive Google Drive Zoho Docs Think Free

Differents Applications

Microsoft Onedrive

With Microsoft Onedrive you can save your files and photos and get them from any device. You can also share files and folders with your family and friends.

Google Drive

With Google Drive you can save any file in the cloud. You will also have access to a wide variety of applications:

  • Docs to edit documents.
  • Sheets to edit spreadsheets.
  • Slides to edit presentations.
  • Keep to save your notes.

Zoho Docs

With Zoho Docs Store everything online. You can conveniently share your files with your friends and family.

Think Free

Think Free is an office package.

  • Word - Wordprocessor.
  • Cell - Spreadsheet.
  • Show - Presentation.
